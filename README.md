# Ember Ascent

A vertical action game where you play as a fire sprite trying to ascend a burning ancient forest before it collapses into ash.

## Gameplay

- **Tap to leap** between glowing branches
- **Collect ember sparks** to refuel your flame (health/fuel)
- **Dodge falling raindrops** that shrink and extinguish you
- Reach the top of each level to advance

## Controls

- **Tap/Click** — Jump to the nearest branch above you
- The higher you climb, the faster the music tempo

## Levels

1. **Sapling** — Gentle introduction
2. **Young Tree** — Picking up the pace
3. **Old Oak** — More hazards appear
4. **Ancient Grove** — Rain becomes frequent
5. **The Crown** — Final ascent to victory
